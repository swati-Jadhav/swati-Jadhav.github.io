Household Shifting Services

YOU NEED IT. WE SHIFT IT

Shifting is always a tedious task. It involves packing, loading, transportation, unloading and unpacking. With so many steps involved, you are bound to struggle some or other way. But what if you have the professional help? What if someone does all the steps for you with professionalism?

We at Cloud Packers are dedicated to offering you the world-class household shifting services. We have years of experience and highly trained professionals who will be at your services. Be it a domestic or international shifting we are there for you.

What we offer you with our household shifting services?

All you have to do is tell us where do you want to move and when do you want to move. We plan your shifting according to your needs. We can also customise our services for every type of items in your household. To give you a better idea, let us give you a brief detail on our household shifting service:

Packing and Unpacking

With our household shifting services, we manage the packing of your belongings. Be it kitchenware, furniture, electronics, clothes, books, antiques etc. We take care of everything. We provide the boxes and other packing material. If you require a specific packing for a valuable item, we can do that for you.

Our employees are well trained in packing. They professionally pack everything and double check every package to make sure nothing is compromised during transportation. Once your belongings are shifted to a new location, we unpack every item. You won’t have to do anything.

Loading and Unloading

We use the most modern and reliable equipment to load and unload your belongings. Even the personnel involved in loading and unloading understand the art of perfect loading. They will make sure that security of your belonging is high.

Transportation

The vehicle we use to transport your belongings is reliable. Safety of your belongings is our priority, and we make sure that whoever is involved in transport is also reliable and experienced in every kind of transportation. We also strive to offer delivery on time. So, you can be assured that your goods be at your new home before you reach there. If you are shifting internationally, we can handle all the permits and papers required for international shipping. We have experience of working with the best shipping companies.
