Our FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How Packers and Movers Work?

Cloud Packers and movers have a very simple 3 step process. First, contact us and our executive will visit and quote you. 2nd file the price and date of moving. In the last step, our professionals will go to your location and pack properly and load your goods in the truck. The last but not on the list is our professionals will unload and unpack your goods at your destination house.

What is the Charges of Packers and Movers?

Cloud Packer's charges are very low and reasonable. Also, a shifting cost totally depends on your total no of goods and total distance from the source to destination. But on average, we charge 10 to 15k for 1 BHK house. Price may vary depends on your goods.

How much time take to deliver my goods?

The delivery of your goods depends on the distance. Cloud packers all the goods by road and the speed of the truck is slow as we give first priority to safety. Example Form Pune to Bangalore a train may take 12 to 24 hours and our truck took 2 to 3 days to reach.

How much does the premove survey cost?

The pre-survey is free of cost. Our supervisor, after taking into account, the quantity of items, distance to be covered, does the calculation and then gives free quotation.

How Do You Dettermine The Price of my Move?

The cost will differ, because it will depend on the quantity of goods. The second is, if the move is local, interstate or international. Sometimes, even the weather conditions also take a part in the price. The charges can come to Rs 5,000 to Rs 10,000 for a single BHK for a local move. Please note, the price given is only approximate.

Why do our supervisors conduct pre-surveys? Are they necessary?

The pre-survey gives undue benefits to everyone who opts for our high quality services. The visit is important because the supervisor takes into account the quantity of goods, professionals needed for packing, loading, unloading and transportation. Our Cloud packers Pune team opt for the survey method for every relocation process – be it, home shifting services, office relocation services, luggage removal services, car and bike transportation services. As a customer, you can use the opportunity to have your doubts clarified about the packing, loading or transportation.

WHO WOULD BE YOUR POINT OF CONTACT IN THE MOVING PROCESS?

A relocation company’s task is not only about only moving the items. It involves taking entire responsibility of the process, facing any challenges to make the move successful. During a home relocation, our packers and movers Pune team understands the emotional bond, your family members share with some household/office items in the loading truck. Even in relocation of offices, you/ management may have some doubts. Similar is the case during car/bike transportation. It is only for these reasons; Cloudpackers give a number – single point of contact. You are welcome to call the number 24/7 to clarify your doubts.

WHAT HAPPENS DURING A LAST MINUTE CHANGE?

Our team of packers and movers in Pune are well-trained to handle any situation with ease. They can accommodate even the last-minute changes and make necessary arrangements. When you get the final invoice, it will not have any prior fees, hidden charges and will only reflect the services given during the process. Please note, we can even make arrangements for a last week move. However, we request you to kindly give us a call at least three days before to make perfect arrangements. If you have already decided to make a move on a day, we will make every necessary arrangement to provide 100% assistance.

DO YOU HAVE A MOVING CHECKLIST I CAN USE FOR SPECIAL REQUESTS?

Yes! We do have. But the requests are many such as moving of garden plants, transportation of vintage cars, fragile items, wooden furniture and more. You can have a consultation with our supervisor during his visit. He can give you the moving checklist as per the need.

CAN I PACK MY OWN THINGS?

You are always welcome. But a humble request – kindly pack the confidential items such as your jewels, mobiles, clothes and other essential items by yourself. For the hard tasks such as packing of wooden bed, iron cot, almirah, leave them to our experienced packers and movers team. By using the best packing materials, our team ensure error-free damage to your items during transportation. Please take note, our supervisor makes a list of items that are to be packed, loaded and unloaded from the truck. Our relocation task ends only after the supervisor, at the new location checks the list for complete delivery of the items.

CAN CLOUDPACKERS HELP STORE SOME OF MY RESIDENTIAL ITEMS IN THEIR WAREHOUSES?

Yes, we can definitely offer assistance in this situation. We also offer temporary storage services for offices, industries, SMBs and corporate organizations. When needed, you are free to relocate the said goods to your home or office or industry.

WHAT TIME DOES YOUR CREW COME TO MY HOME/OFFICE?

Usually, the Pune team starts their work at 9 AM. There are occasions, when the customer has asked us to come a bit early to avoid traffic congestion on the road. The last timings of the packing and unloading crew end at 5 PM. This is to ensure our employees also reach on time to their homes. Only under certain circumstances or emergencies, do we allow the unloading to be conducted after 6 PM.

IS MY PRESENCE REQUIRED DURING THE PACKING PROCESS OR UNLOADING THE ITEMS?

You can grace the packing process with your own presence. Or else, you can ask your spouse or family member to be on your behalf. The same condition applies when unloading and delivering the items. Only after the paperwork gets completed, will our Pune movers and packers team move from your place.
