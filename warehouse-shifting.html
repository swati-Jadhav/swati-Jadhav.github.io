Warehouse Shifting Services

YOU NEED IT. WE SHIFT IT

Having a world-class storage and warehousing strategy is an important factor for the success of a business in the present day environment. We offer you world class inventory and superior delivery services that meet your business requirements. With our reliable warehouses in Ambala, Dharuhera, Palwal, Gurgaon, Jaipur, and Bangalore, we give you far more than just space. You get a dedicated warehouse solution and years of experience in the industry that will serve your supply chain.

What Does Our Warehouse Solution Offer?

Our warehouse solution offers a secure, clean and dry storage space with services like:

  • Inventory Management
  • Goods Handling
  • Stretch Wrap
  • Labelling
  • Distribution and Logistics
  • Pallet Services
  • And much more

Our Warehouses can safely store goods like electronic appliances, computers, mattresses, documents, antiques, furnishing, instruments, clothes and much more. We offer storage services for these goods for any specified time frame you want. Our storage facilities have state of the art security to make sure that client doesn’t worry about their goods and just focus on meeting the needs of the supply chain. In addition, we have well-trained professionals who provide 24X7 supports to clients, so that the efficiency of a client’s business is increased and management time is cut down.

Who Can Use Our Services?

Our storage and warehousing services serve all kinds of businesses and Individuals. You can either be a cooperate or commercial or individual user we service them all with equal vigour. Wherever the client requires us to ship the goods, we do it with efficiency and within a time frame. We arrange for the shipment during both export from and import to the specified destination. In addition, we make sure the person handling your goods is fully experienced and trustworthy.
