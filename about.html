About Cloud Packers and Movers

YOU NEED IT. WE SHIFT IT

We have movers and packers services all over India and globally, and we can pick any type of goods right from the doorstep and transport securely with all grace, luxury to the scheduled location. In case of domestic long route transportation, you will look for nominal charges where your goods are stored in the safest way possible and transported properly to the perfect location. We employ international state-of-the-art resources and since we know that each order will bring a different set of challenges, our experienced team is properly equipped to meet any situation.

We make use of cellophane sheets, bubble wraps, waterproof cartons, corrugated boxes as well as cello tapes for your items and ensure they are sent in carriers which have locks, seals. Our team follows the transparent process every time and our customer executives have all the answers to your queries/concerns.

Our services go beyond your expectations and for every assignment, we work to the limit to make your relocation hassles stress-free, national or international. To be known and respected as an International Relocation partner for every customer. To ensure that every customer retains his/her faith in the relocation services of every company in the globe. In fact, they should think shifting of goods is the most hassle-free job in the world.

Procedure

We have a multi-city presence. A clear strategy is planned for each assignment and ensured that not even a minute item does get ignored to prevent flawless and in-time delivery. When we agree to an assignment, our relocation team will meet you for conducting the preliminary analysis. Package of the goods is then done, and notes of delicate items, expensive stuff and special consignments are taken note of.

We understand the above statement and never charge extra for the services not offered by us. We are owners to quality vehicles (heavy-duty trucks as well as lorries) so it becomes easy to transport your goods devoid of damage. We also have tracking systems and monitoring mechanisms of our transport drivers

We know customer care is the representative of our company and our executives are selected after a rigorous round of interviews. Also, they are provided training so as to give the best possible response. The status of the transported material is always a cause of concern to you and keeping this aspect in mind, we have a 24/7 customer care. You can connect to us via multiple tools such as chat, text, email, online presence and telephonic support. Because of our national presence, you can contact any customer care executive and they will be able to provide you the perfect data regarding your goods.

As a packer and mover company, we have always followed the dictates of every State. There will be regulations that have to be fulfilled prior to taking any shifting of goods. Our team ensures that every policy is followed so that you do not have to worry about road permits, transit insurance and taxes. If you have a requirement, give us a chance to provide our value-based services by just making a call or send us an email at info@cloudpackers.com
